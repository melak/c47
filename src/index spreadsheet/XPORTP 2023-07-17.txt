Any further caracters not in the ASCII set is translater according to a few rules:



All arrow types are consolidated to ->, <-, v, ^, type arrows
Double arrows are translated to >> type arrows
i, j, binary 1 and 0 converted to ASCII
All separators are converted to comma, period or apostrophe
All space sizes converted to single space
All superscripts and subscripts are converted to standard numbers/text
    STD_SUP_0    -  STD_SUP_9     converted to 0 - 9
    STD_SUP_a    -  STD_SUP_z     converted to a - z
    STD_SUP_A    -  STD_SUP_Z     converted to A - Z
    STD_SUB_0    -  STD_SUB_9     converted to 0 - 9
    STD_SUB_a    -  STD_SUB_z     converted to a - z
    STD_SUB_A    -  STD_SUB_Z     converted to A - Z
    STD_BASE_1   -  STD_BASE_16   converted to #1 - #16

All quotes are converted to standard ' and "
